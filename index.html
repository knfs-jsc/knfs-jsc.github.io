Our Story

On April 1, 2021, KNFs was founded by two Vietnamese software engineers with a strong passion for information technology. We aspire to make information technology a solution for life. Believing firmly that technology should serve people, we focus on efficiency and optimization, steering clear of any frills or complications.

KNFs stands for Kent and Friends. With us, everyone is a friend, united in developing the information technology industry. Join us on the journey to bring information technology to every place and every field.

We always believe in the information technology capabilities of Vietnamese people. Vietnamese engineers constantly strive and strive to bring information technology to everywhere
